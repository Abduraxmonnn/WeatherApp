Be Smart! Be Skilled! Be Professional!

 

      Within the Korean-Uzbek business forum dedicated to the state visit of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan, Shavkat Mirziyoyev, to the Repubic of Korea on the 22-24th November 2017, a Memorandm on the creation of Yeoju Technical Institute in Tashkent was signed between Korean and Uzbek partners. Based on this Memorandum, the Yeoju Technical Institute in Tashkent received the status of an official institution providing educational services in the field of higher education in the Republic of Uzbekistan on the basis of a state license granted by the State Inspectorate for Quality Supervision of Education under the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan on July 24, 2018.

      The main task of Yeoju Technical Institute in Tashkent is the preparation of highly qualified personnel and specialists with the modern professional skills demanded in the job market. In this regard, curricula are formed taking into account modern requirements and the tendencies of the development of the economy as well as those agreed upon with basic and potential employers.

      The institute has modern facilities and along a constantly-updated information-resource database. Overall, all conditions for students’ obtaining quality education and having leisure time in the institute are in place. International professors from the UK, the USA, Belgium, South Korea, Saudi Arabia, etc. are involved in the educational process and the national team of the teaching staff is mostly formed from the graduates of leading overseas institutes of the UK, the USA, Italy, Germany, Japan, South Korea, etc.

      The students have an opportunity to participate in exchange programmes as well as in programmes on receiving joint or double degrees. This way the students may participate in exchange programmes on the 2+2 model and continue their education at Hanyang University, Chungbuk National University, Hankyong University, Chosun University, Busan University of Foreign studies, Jeonju University, Chungcheong university (Republic of Korea), United Business Institutes (Belgium) and Yeoju Institute of Technology. Thus, Yeoju Technical Institute in Tashkent offers great opportunities for obtaining quality education in an international and friendly atmosphere.
